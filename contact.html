What we're glad to receive

  • Corrections — spot something wrong or out of date? Tell us; we'd rather fix it than defend it.
  • Local knowledge — festival dates, road conditions, a fonda worth recommending.
  • Photos and stories — especially of the Loma Ranchito area and the Herrera highlands, from residents and families with roots there.
  • Questions — planning a trip to the region and can't find an answer? Ask; if we don't know, we may know who does.

We read everything, though replies can take a few days. Please don't send anything you don't want published without telling us — if you share photos or stories, say whether we may use them on the site, and how to credit you.
