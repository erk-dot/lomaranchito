How we handle data on Lomaranchito.com

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

This website, Lomaranchito.com, is an independent informational guide about Loma Ranchito, a small community in Herrera Province, Panama. We are committed to being transparent about how this site operates with respect to data and your privacy.

No Personal Data Collection

Lomaranchito.com does not directly collect, store, or process any personal information from visitors. We do not operate contact forms, user accounts, email sign-ups, or any mechanism that gathers your name, email address, or other identifying information.

Google Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. Google Analytics collects information about how visitors use the site — such as which pages are visited, time spent on the site, and general geographic location based on IP address. This data is aggregated and anonymous, and helps us understand how the site is used so we can improve it.

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this information. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. For more information on how Google uses data, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

Google AdSense Advertising

Lomaranchito.com plans to display advertisements served by Google AdSense, an advertising service provided by Google LLC. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other sites on the internet. This allows Google and its partners to show you relevant advertisements.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings. For more information, see the Google Advertising Policies.

Cookies & Third-Party Tracking

This site uses cookies placed by third-party services — specifically Google Analytics and Google AdSense — as described above. These cookies are managed by Google, not by Lomaranchito.com directly. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on this and other websites.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to any parties beyond the third-party services explicitly described in this policy.

External Links

This site may contain links to external websites, including social media pages. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.
