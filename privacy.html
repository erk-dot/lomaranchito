Privacy Policy

What this site collects, why, and the choices you have. Last updated: July 17, 2026.

Home › Privacy Policy

Who we are

lomaranchito.com is an independent, personally maintained informational website about Loma Ranchito and the Herrera–Azuero region of Panama. For anything in this policy, you can reach us at info@lomaranchito.com.

What we collect directly

Nothing, by default. This site has no accounts, no comment system, and no forms. If you email us, we receive whatever you choose to send (your address and message) and use it only to reply and act on your message. We don't sell or share your correspondence.

Analytics (Google Analytics 4)

We use Google Analytics to understand, in aggregate, how the site is used — which pages are read, roughly where visitors come from, and what devices they use. Google Analytics sets cookies and collects usage data (such as pages viewed, approximate location derived from IP, and device information) on our behalf. We use this information only to improve the site. You can opt out with the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Advertising (Google AdSense)

This site displays ads served by Google AdSense to cover its costs. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. In particular:

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.
  • You can opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising at www.aboutads.info/choices.

Depending on your location, you may see a consent message before ads or measurement cookies are set, and you can change your choice at any time through that message.

Cookies

Cookies are small files stored by your browser. On this site they come from the two services above — analytics and advertising. You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings; the site works fine without them.

Links to other sites

Pages here link to external sites (for example, Facebook and official park or tourism resources). Their privacy practices are their own; this policy covers only lomaranchito.com.

Children

This site is a general-audience travel and culture guide and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example, the EU/EEA or California), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data held about you. For anything within our direct control (i.e., email correspondence), write to info@lomaranchito.com. For data held by Google, use the links above or Google's own privacy tools at policies.google.com/privacy.

Changes to this policy

If we change how the site handles data — for example, by adding a newsletter or comments — this page will be updated first, with a new "last updated" date at the top.
